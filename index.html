SSC Official Requirements

  • File Size: Must be strictly between 10 KB and 20 KB.
  • Dimensions: 6.0 cm width × 2.0 cm height (recommended) or 4.0 cm × 2.0 cm.
  • Resolution: 300 DPI for high print clarity.
  • Format: JPEG/JPG files only.
  • Background: Unblurred, plain white background with black or blue ink signature.

Why SSC Rejects Signatures

The SSC registration portal automatically rejects uploads that fall even slightly outside specifications (e.g. 9.9 KB or 20.1 KB). Additionally, capturing a photo with a smartphone often results in a grey/shadowed background, which causes manual verification failure. This formatter removes background shadows and processes the image locally to fit precisely within the 10-20 KB requirement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the whitening feature work?

It filters grey tints, shadows, and yellow room lighting from your photo, turning the paper pure white while enhancing your signature's ink contrast.

Is my signature secure?

Absolutely. All processing and compression runs locally on your device using client-side JavaScript. Your signature is never uploaded to any server or database.

What if my file is still under 10 KB?

If your signature is very short or thin, the optimized file might fall below 10 KB. If this happens, we recommend signing with a slightly thicker black or blue pen, or capturing the photo with a bit more detail, and converting it again.
