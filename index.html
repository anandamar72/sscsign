SSC Official Requirements

  • File Size: Must be strictly between 10 KB and 20 KB.
  • Dimensions: 6.0 cm width × 2.0 cm height (recommended ratio).
  • Resolution: 300 DPI (Dots Per Inch) for optimal print quality.
  • Format: JPEG/JPG extension files only.
  • Ink & Background: Plain white background with clear black or blue ink signature.

Why SSC Rejects Uploads

The Staff Selection Commission registration system strictly scans uploaded signature files. Common reasons for rejection include file sizes falling even slightly outside limits (e.g., 9.8 KB or 20.2 KB), or background shadows/yellow light from home photography. This tool whitens paper backdrops, auto-scales physical DPI coordinates, and runs a compression loop to output files strictly in bounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the whitening contrast work?

It filters gray tones, phone shadows, and low indoor room lighting, making paper backgrounds clean white while keeping your signature strokes high contrast.

Is my signature secure and private?

Yes. All canvas calculations and compression run locally inside your browser client-side. Your signature data never travels across a network or server.

What if my file size is still below 10 KB?

If your signature is very thin or small, the output size might fall under 10 KB. Use the Contrast slider to decrease whitening aggressiveness, or sign with a slightly thicker ink pen and capture again.
